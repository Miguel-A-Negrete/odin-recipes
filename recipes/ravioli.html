Description

homemade ravioli is a project. A pasta project. Do not attempt on a Tuesday night after work or soccer practice (or both). It takes time.

Repetition. A glass of wine perhaps? Once you find a groove, the process will become surprisingly therapeutic and the results: absolutely spectacular.

Ingredients

Pasta dough

  • Tipo 00 Flour
  • Eggs + Egg yolk
  • Olive oil

Ravioli cheese filling

  • Whole Milk Ricotta
  • Mascarpone cheese
  • Fresh Basil
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Egg

Preparation Steps

  1. Make Pasta Dough
  2. Make Ravioli Filling
  3. Roll Pasta Dough Into Sheets
  4. Assemble & Cut Ravioli
  5. Plunge 1-2 ravioli into a pot of boiling water- make sure it's generously salted. Cook for 3-6 minutes or more
